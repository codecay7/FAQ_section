Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Our standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days within the INDIA.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! You’ll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping times vary based on the destination.

What if my order is delayed or doesn’t arrive within the estimated timeframe?

While we strive to meet our shipping estimates, occasionally delays can occur due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g., weather, customs, or carrier issues). If your order is significantly delayed, please reach out to our customer support team, and we’ll investigate and provide updates
